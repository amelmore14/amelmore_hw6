Who I Am

My name is Allison Elmore and I am a senior at the University of Iowa. In May, 2025 I will graduate with a degree in Business Analytics and Information Systems. Although school is a big part of my life, there is so much more to me than that. I currently work with the University of Iowa Health Care on the strategy team as their student assistant.

When I am not at school or work I enjoy being outside if I can. I love to go on walks, hike, read, and just sit in nature. My schedule this semester gives me so much free time where I can spend all my afternoons outside if I wanted to.

One big thing about me is I love to travel. I have been very blessed growing up to have a family who travels together each year, typically to somewhere out of the country. Since I was in 5th grade we have taken a trip each year that includes myself, mom, dad, sister, aunt, grandma and grandpa. Not a lot of people get these opportunites in life and I soak in each and every one of them. This year for my graduation trip we are taking a week long cruise in Alaska!
